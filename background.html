Background and Historical Context

Colonial Impact : Forced Removal

Many Native people live a lifestyle between state and tribal lands. Due to forced removal, only 22% live on reservations. Due to this a variety of issues like reporting policies, jurisdictional issues, and communication are present

Colonial Impact : Residential Schools

In Indian boarding schools, many Indigenous people, especially women were sexually and physically abused by institution leaders. These institutions covered up abuse which set a precedent of abuse and neglect that continues today. Due to this, violence and ignorance toward Indigenous peoples has become normalized
