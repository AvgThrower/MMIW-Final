Current Issue / Contributing Factors

Stats / Data : Disparities

  • On some reservations women are murdered at a rate greater than 10 times the national average
  • Over 80% of Indigenous people have experienced violence in their life
  • 2.5x more likely to experience violent crimes
  • 2x more likely to experience rape / sexual assault

Jurisdictional Issues : Tribal vs Federal

Federal laws regarding violent crimes on Native land with non-Native perpetrators create difficulties within prosecution. The FBI is responsible for serious crimes, but Tribal police are law enforcement on Native Land.
